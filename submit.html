WANT TO SUBMIT JOURNALS?

Once you’ve chosen the right journal for your research and written your paper, it’s time to make your submission. Read on for information to help guide you through the whole process of article submission.

  1. Visit the relevant sites (provided below) to check the specific submission requirements for your chosen journal.
  2. When you submit your article to a journal, your cover letter gives you the opportunity to highlight to the journal’s editor why they should consider publishing your research.
